About Me

I am a Research Fellow in Computational Statistics at Bocconi University. My research interests primarily focus on the theory and methodology of Markov chain Monte Carlo. I have contributed to the development of Monte Carlo methods driven by piecewise deterministic Markov chains and I am currently interested in parallelization strategies for Bayesian computation and Heavy-tailed sampling. Prior to this position, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Warwick, UK (2023–2024), and I obtained my PhD at Delft University of Technology (2018–2023).
